About Daniela Araujo

Daughter of Brazilian Christian music artists, her father, Jorge Araújo , renowned singer and music producer, and her mother, Eula Paula, pop / Pentecostal singer, Daniela spent her childhood and adolescence in studios, recordings and shows. Along with his brothers, Daniela Araújo joined the group Noise of the Class , which achieved great success in the country during the decade of 90 . In 2006, in the rehearsals for the recording of the vocal of the DVD Deus de Promessas Ao Vivo , from Toque Toque Altar , he met Leonardo Gonçalves , also a singer, who later became his husband.According to Daniela, this played a key role in shaping his first eponymous album Daniela Araújo , which was released in 2011 by Sony Music Brazil . The album, which appealed to both critics and the general public, earned him a nomination for "Revelation of the Year" in the Promises 2012 Trophy . In 2013 she began the production of her second career album titled Creator of the World , released in May 2014. It was also in 2013 that Daniela signed her production partnership with her brother Jorginho Araújo, initially working on the production of the album Geração de Jesus , of Jotta a . In 2014 he has worked with the CD production of Priscila Alcântara , Brenda, Bekah Costa and Gabriela Rocha . Daniela Araújo also launched a clothing store with her own style called "Da Bazar". Born and raised in the Assembly of God, today the singer belongs to Baptist Church . Daniela was married to the singer Adventist Leonardo Gonçalves since 2009. In December of 2015 , Daniela announced the separation between her and the singer through a video posted on her social network.
